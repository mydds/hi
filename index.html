Hi I’m Danny. I grew up on the other side of the globe & have since lived, traveled, and worked in 130+ countries around the world, each of which has uniquely shaped my life. I have a business background, but my work has spanned some pretty wide-ranging areas — I've led operations for global humanitarian responses, launched new business ventures in emerging markets, and built tech platforms to connect international teams across every continent ..among other things.  After over a decade leading various business + development initiatives, I began a personal project around the world, with a simple goal to leave each community I met along the way just a little bit better. The reality of this journey blew away even my wildest expectations — from working with tribes in the mountains of Ethiopia, to sustainability initiatives in Vanuatu, to social entrepreneurship projects in Indonesia it was filled with some of the most meaningful experiences of my life. It also showed me the power of technology to create opportunities in even the most remote parts of the world, and inspired me to explore these possibilities even further. Along the way I've been doing some consulting, some development, and some startup advising+investing – while on the side I’m also building some experimental projects to simplify emerging tech for wider communities. Outside of work I’ve held a lifelong love of exploration and photography, which I try to capture + share as much as possible. I always like meeting people who are doing things against the grain, particularly in pursuit of big, crazy dreams — if you're struggling, need some help, or just want to chat over some new ideas don't hesitate to reach out and say hello.
